Our Story

A small cart, a big God, and a whole lot of espresso.

We're a couple who first fell in love with Jesus, and then with each other — and somewhere along the way, with the quiet ritual of a perfectly pulled shot. 5 Sparrows is named after a single verse: "Are not five sparrows sold for two pennies? Yet not one of them is forgotten by God." — Luke 12:6.

That's the heart of every drink we serve. You're seen. You're remembered. And your wedding, your launch party, your church potluck — it's worth showing up beautifully for.

"It was never just coffee for us."

We hand-craft every syrup in our kitchen, dial in our espresso each morning, and roll our cart anywhere from a Hoboken wedding to a Chanel launch in Manhattan. A trailer is on the way for 2026.
